# Pal-Plant: Core Identity & Philosophy

## 1. The Vision
**"Relationships are living organisms, not static data."**
Pal-Plant is designed to shift the user's perspective from "contact management" (productivity) to "relationship nurturing" (vitality). By using a garden metaphor, the app visualizes the abstract nature of human connection as a living system that requires consistent care to prevent "wilting".

## 2. Identity Pillars

* **The Garden Metaphor**
    * **The Mechanism:** Relationships are categorized into lifecycles: *Thriving*, *Growing*, *Sprouting*, *Wilting*, and *Withered*.
    * **The Purpose:** This provides immediate visual feedback on relationship health. A "Withered" connection is marked with a skull icon, signaling a critical need for outreach.

* **Frequency-Weighted Intentionality**
    * **The Mechanism:** The "Social Garden Score" (0–100) is not a simple average. It uses a frequency-weighted formula that prioritizes high-maintenance relationships.
    * **The Purpose:** This acknowledges that different people require different cadences. A friend you wish to contact every 3 days has a higher impact on your global garden health than a distant relative contacted once a year.

* **The "Sweet Spot" Philosophy**
    * **The Mechanism:** The scoring system rewards interactions made when 0–50% of a contact timer remains.
    * **The Purpose:** It discourages "over-watering" (contacting too early, which incurs a penalty) and neglect (contacting when overdue). This promotes sustainable, healthy social boundaries.

* **Chronological Tiering (Momentum vs. Foundation)**
    * **The Mechanism:** Individual friend scores are calculated using a Three-Tier model: **Momentum** (0–30 days), **Consistency** (31–89 days), and **Foundation** (90–730 days).
    * **The Purpose:** This reflects human psychology; long-term bonds (Foundation) provide stability, while new or active connections (Momentum) drive current relationship energy.

## 3. Voice and Tone
* **Nurturing but Honest:** The UI uses soft themes like "Forest" and "Sunset," but remains technically rigorous. If a user neglects a "plant," the system applies a **Daily Wilt** penalty (-2 points per day late) to the global score.
* **Privacy-First:** The app's identity is rooted in trust. All data, analytics, and contact logs are stored locally on the device (IndexedDB), ensuring the "Garden" remains a private space.

## 4. Summary Statement
**Pal-Plant** is a local-first relationship garden that uses botanical metaphors and time-weighted scoring to help users visualize and maintain their social health. It honors the long-term foundations of friendship while rewarding the intentional "sweet spot" of consistent connection.
